In this research, I applied AI machine learning methods to integrate multiple geophysical datasets—including gravity, gravity gradiometry, magnetic, electromagnetic (VTEM), and radiometric data—from the Iron Horse area in Churchill Falls, Labrador. The goal was to generate a 2D geological map and a 3D geological model of the region. We focused on three main lithological units of the study area: iron formation, gabbro, and sediments. These three lithological groups are closely linked to mineralization, making them ideal targets for geophysical exploration. The map below shows the location of the study area.
